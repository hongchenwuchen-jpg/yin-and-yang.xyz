About the Game

Yin and Yang is a relaxing puzzle game that challenges you to reunite the forces of light and dark. It combines simple controls with clever levels, making it enjoyable for both casual and experienced players.

The goal is to bring the Yin and Yang together by removing obstacles and solving logical puzzles. Every level is designed with harmony in mind, offering a calm yet engaging experience on both desktop and mobile devices.

Game Features

  • Beautiful minimalist design inspired by Eastern philosophy
  • Relaxing gameplay with smooth difficulty progression
  • Optimized for both PC and mobile browsers
